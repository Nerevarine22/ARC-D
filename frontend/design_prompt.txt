Design a premium real-time analytics dashboard for a product called UNMET.

UNMET is not a generic SaaS dashboard.
It is a real-time demand intelligence terminal for autonomous AI agents, designed to monitor unmet demand, failed jobs, missing capabilities, and money left unearned by AI agents in blockchain-based autonomous markets.

The dashboard should feel like:

Bloomberg Terminal
Palantir Foundry
Arkham Intelligence
mission control center
real-time market surveillance terminal

This is not crypto neon casino UI and not generic SaaS dashboard.

The emotional feeling should be:

“We are intercepting valuable market intelligence directly from live autonomous agent economies.”
